Why Am I Here?

Do you keep hearing the word BitCoin and feel like you're missing out? Do you wish someone could teach you how to get started? You are NOT ALONE!

Zack hears this story all the time from friends, familiy, and people on the street.

After Close to 4 years following and exploring digital currency, he makes it SO EASY for you to understand.

What Will I Learn?

  • What is a BitCoin?

    Zack explains the basics of digital currency in a simple, easy to follow, way that will make sense to everyone. You do not need to know anything about computers, code, or programming to understand Bitocin. In this overview you'll learn exactly how Digital Currency works without having to hear computer jargon like "Crypotgraphy" and "Hash" over and over again.

  • How Can I Use BitCoin?

    BitCoin is a form of money. You can spend it to buy things or hold on to it for the future. You'll learn how to buy, earn, and spend BitCoin.

  • How Do I Save BitCoin?

    BitCoin is very easy to have and use. Zack shows you exactly how to best store your BitCoins whether you want easy access to spend them, or want to buy and securely store them in your safe deposit box.

What Do I Get?

In addition to learning everything you'll need to know to buy and spend BitCoin, you will actually take some with you.

You may have heard the term "Alt Coin" but you may know them better as LiteCoin or Etherium. In your wallet by the end of the session you will have more than just BitCoin.
